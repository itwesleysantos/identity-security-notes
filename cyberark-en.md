CyberArk is a security platform primarily focused on **protecting identities** (human and machine) to prevent cyber threats. The company's core is **Privileged Access Management (PAM)**, which involves controlling user access to the most critical systems and sensitive data.

CyberArk's platform offers specialized solutions for complex environments with high-security requirements (such as in the financial and healthcare sectors):

* **Privileged Access Management (PAM):** This is the central focus. The goal is to protect and monitor accounts with elevated privileges, utilizing a **"Vault"** to isolate and secure credentials and passwords.
* **Identity Security:** Covers the management of the identity lifecycle, including the adoption of measures such as Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and Single Sign-On (SSO).
* **Secrets Management (DevSecOps):** Offers centralized storage and management for technical secrets (API keys, certificates) used by applications and in the development workflow.
* **Cloud Security:** Specialized in protecting identities and workloads within cloud infrastructures (such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud).

CyberArk distinguishes itself through its unified approach to security and the core technology it employs:
