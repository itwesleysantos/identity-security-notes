# ‚òÅÔ∏è ServiceNow: Workflow Management Platform 

ServiceNow is a **cloud-based digital platform** designed to **automate, organize, and manage internal processes** across companies of all sizes. Initially focused on IT, today it serves areas like HR, Finance, Legal, and Customer Service.

Its main objective is to **digitize business workflows**, replacing manual tasks (such as approvals via email or spreadsheets) with automated and efficient processes.

---

## üß© Key System Functions

The system operates in **independent modules** that can be activated like "LEGO blocks," adapting to the specific needs of each department:

* **IT Service Management (ITSM):** The core module for **managing IT services** (calls, incidents, problems, and changes).
* **HR Service Delivery (HRSD):** Automates HR processes, such as **vacation requests, employee onboarding, and offboarding**.
* **Customer Service Management (CSM):** Focused on **customer support**, contract management, and high-level service automation.
* **IT Operations Management (ITOM):** Monitors the **IT infrastructure in real-time** and automates tasks to prevent problems.
* **Security Operations (SecOps) and GRC:** Dedicated to **digital security** (incident response) and **compliance with internal and external rules and regulations**.
* **App Creation:** Allows users to build **custom applications from scratch** using the **App Engine Studio** tool.

---

## üí° Features and Differentiators

ServiceNow is a leading tool chosen by major corporations (such as Google and Microsoft) due to:

* **Low-Code Nature:** Allows **anyone to create flows and applications** with visual tools (Flow Designer, UI Builder) without needing to code.
* **Scalability and Scope:** It is capable of **unifying process management** across the entire company, from IT to Legal.
* **Integration:** Easily connects with other systems and APIs (using IntegrationHub) to automate complex tasks.
